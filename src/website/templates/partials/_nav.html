About this website

This website is for viewing historical grade distributions from various colleges at Texas A&M University. Search for records through colleges, professors, or particular courses.

Professional colleges are not included in this database. Additionally, certain semesters do not have records of grade distributions.

The grade distribution data comes from the Office of the Registrar from Texas A&M University. All data used for this webpage was publicly accessed from the registrar.

In the interests of FERPA (Family Education Rights and Privacy Act) and the protection of student privacy, course sections with an enrollment of less than five are not included in the reports.

This website is self-maintaining, so as soon as future records are submitted by the registrar, the database will also be automatically updated.

This service is not affiliated with any part of Texas A&M University.
